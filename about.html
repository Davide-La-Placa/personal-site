Personality & passions

A few fun facts

Ciao and welcome to Davide La placa world! I'm working in my family business, an Italian Pizzeria Bakery, dishing out delicious slices of passion and software engineer dreams! When I'm not tossing dough or debugging code, you'll often find me in the gym. I believe in the power of a good workout to fuel both body and mind. Plus, it helps me burn off all the pizza and pastries I taste-test in the name of quality control (it's a tough job, but someone has to do it).

Speaking of pizza, let's talk about my legendary pies. You see, making pizza isn't just a job for me; it's a passion. I've honed my craft over the years, perfecting the delicate dance between sauce, cheese, and topping. Our oven brings out flavor you won't find anywhere else, creating a slice of heaven that'll make your taste buds do the happy dance. Trust me, once you've had a taste, you'll be forever spoiled. Now, you might be wondering why a pizza maestro like me decided to dive into the world of software development. Well, it turns out that creating a perfect pizza is a lot like coding: it requires precision, attention to detail, and a dash of creativity. By blending my two passions, I'm on a mission to serve up not just tasty slices, but also innovative solutions that'll make your digital craving disappear. So, whether you're here for a mouthwatering slice of pizza, a delightful pastry, or some tech talk over an espresso, I got you covered. Join us on this delicious journey, where culinary artistry meets software sorcery. We can't wait to make you a part of our ever-growing family.
Buon appetito and happy coding!
